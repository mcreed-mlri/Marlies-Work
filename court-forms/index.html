What changed in the redesign
🗣️
Plainer words
Shorter sentences. "Good news — you don't have to meet the work rules" instead of "You appear to be exempt."
🖼️
A picture on every question
Icons carry the meaning before the words do, which helps new readers.
👆
Bigger targets
Large Yes / No tiles and chunky answer rows, easier to read and tap.
