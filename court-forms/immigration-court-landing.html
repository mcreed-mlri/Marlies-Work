Is this the right tool for you?

Check the two boxes below.

✓ Use this tool if…
  • ✓You are a lawyer or a person allowed to represent someone.
  • ✓The case is in Immigration Court.
  • ✓You will speak for this person at a hearing.
✕ Do not use it if…
  • ✕Your case is at the Board of Immigration Appeals (BIA).
  • ✕You are not allowed to represent someone in court.
Not sure? Find the right form →

What you need before you start

Have these 4 things nearby.

1
Their name and address
The person you will represent, plus their A-number.
2
The court location
Which Immigration Court is hearing the case.
3
Your EOIR ID number
Your ID from the court system.
4
Your contact info
Your phone, email, and firm or group name.
💡

If you plan to file online (ECAS), first make sure you are signed up with the EOIR eRegistry.

How it works

Three simple steps.

💬
1. Answer questions
We ask a few easy questions. You just type the answers.
📄
2. Get your form
We fill out Form EOIR-28 for you. It is ready to use.
✍️
3. Sign and file
Sign the form and send it to the Immigration Court.
